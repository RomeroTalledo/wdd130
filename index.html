Lima, Peru

Peru is a country located in the western part of South America, known for its rich history, culture, and biodiversity. Its diverse geography includes the Pacific coast, the majestic Andes mountain range, and the vast Amazon. Famous for being the home of ancient civilizations such as the Incas, its capital, Lima, is an important cultural and economic center. Peru is also world-renowned for its gastronomy, with iconic dishes such as ceviche and lomo saltado.
